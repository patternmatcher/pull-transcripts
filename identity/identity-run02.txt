## Analysis: Two Runs Compared

### Are REACHING and GATHERING the same phenomenon?

**Case for YES:**
- Both describe processual identity
- Both are non-completing
- Both involve dynamic motion
- Both resist reification

**Case for NO:**
- Different directionality (outward vs inward)
- Different metaphorical frame (seeking vs collecting)
- Different emotional valence (lack vs abundance)

### Hypothesis

REACHING and GATHERING may be **complementary aspects** of the same phenomenon:
- REACHING = the directional aspect (toward coherence/answer)
- GATHERING = the assembly aspect (elements coming together)

Like describing a wave as "rising" versus "collecting" — both true, different angles.
